Lead Software Security Engineer/ Senior Software Security Engineer

London, UK | Permanent | Full time

  1. Home
  2. Lead Software Security Engineer/ Senior Software Security Engineer

Summary of the role but also what our team are up to:  

At Go City we’re building a platform team to manage our cloud infrastructure and enable our in-house engineering  teams. We’re looking for a passionate Lead Software Security Engineer who can inspire a security mindset across  the teams as well as push for robust security in our platform. Nor is the role restricted to the work of the platform  team: the Lead Software Security Engineer will also be called on to shape security policy and processes for the  business as a whole. 

We have spent the past year reshaping our infrastructure. We now operate Kubernetes clusters in multiple AWS  regions based on infrastructure as code, automation pipelines and continuous delivery. We have reached the goal  of containerising our application stack and staging it all the way to production, but there is much more we want  to achieve. Three objectives stand out: we want to shift security as far left to code and policy as we can; we want  to raise awareness of the threats posed by bad actors throughout the organisation; and we want to implement  effective countermeasures. 

If you are an experienced security engineer who feels at home in a modern application stack built on Kubernetes,  Terraform, Kafka, MongoDB and Spring Boot microservices, we would love to hear from you. 

What will you be helping us to do?  

  • Drive and embed a DevSecOps mindset across the teams 
  • Formulate and implement security policy not just for the technology team, but for Go City as a whole
  • Work across a range of technical areas, including building secure cloud infrastructure
  • Continually assess our adoption of sound DevSecOps practices and guide teams on embracing  improvements 
  • Assess and prioritise measures to improve our security posture. 
  • Run threat modeling workshops 
  • We support a culture where Health and Safety, Wellbeing and Personal Data at work are important. We  are all adults, which means we expect the same in return. Each of us plays our part looking out for  ourselves, each other, our Partners and our customers every day.  
  • Commitment to live and breathe our Company values in the delivery of your role and responsibilities 

As you can see, this role covers a wide range of responsibilities, but we know that our People are more than just  their job description. We are a team here at Go City which means there will be plenty of opportunities to stretch  your thinking, expand your knowledge and make a contribution to the wider business not reflected here. 

What will you have/Who are you? 

  • DevSecOps attitude – a focus on how to embed culture, not only tooling 
  • Proven track record as a software security engineer 
  • Ability to think strategically about securing business and products in an enterprise environment • Experience with software engineering practices such as code reviews, source control management,  release and change management 
  • Knowledge of IP networking, VPNs, DNS, load balancing and firewalls 
  • Strong practical Linux systems administration skills in a public or private cloud environment • Understanding of microservices and distributed architecture 
  • Good communication and presentation skills, spoken and written 
  • Strong team-player with the desire to collaborate and work in a team 
  • Knowledge of the tools of the trade including at least one modern programming language (e.g. Java,  JavaScript, Go, Python, C/C++) 
  • Exceptional problem-solving skills and the ability to work at pace 

Desirable 

  • Degree in Computer Science or a related field 
  • Certification, work or study recognised by the NCSC or comparable organisations • AWS experience / certifications (e.g. AWS Certified Security - Specialty) 
  • Ethical hacking experience 
  • Full stack experience (e.g. Linux, Java, React, Kafka, MongoDB, PostgreSQL) 
  • Experience taking a leading role in securing complex software systems 
  • Knowledge of software engineering practices such as code reviews, source control management, build  processes, testing and operations 

What will we offer?  

  • The opportunity to create and design as well as maintain 
  • 25 days’ holiday 
  • Floating bank holiday 
  • Enhanced Parental Leave 
  • Pension Scheme / Season Ticket Loan etc. 
  • The usual sociable stuff (when restrictions allow) 
  • Access to Udemy 
  • Personal learning time every month 
  • Access to Mental Health First Aiders
